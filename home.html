What do we do

We develop a specialized solution for comparing product prices between GeM (Government e-Marketplace) and other e-commerce platforms. Our system employs web scraping and API integration techniques to gather and organize pricing data from various sources. With a user-friendly interface, we enable users to effortlessly compare prices across platforms, ensuring transparency and competitiveness. This service empowers government departments, organizations, and consumers to make well-informed purchasing decisions, promoting fair and cost-effective procurement practices.
